Privacy Policy for RiAtlas Task

Last updated: July 2026

RiAtlas Task respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

RiAtlas Task does not collect, store, or share personal information from users.

All tasks, categories, reminders, and app settings are stored locally on your device using local storage. This data is not sent to any external server.

Data Storage

The application stores information locally on your device to provide features such as creating tasks, managing categories, tracking completion status, and saving preferences.

Third-Party Services

RiAtlas Task does not use third-party services that collect personal user data.

Permissions

If the application requests permissions (such as notifications for reminders), those permissions are only used to provide app functionality.

Children's Privacy

RiAtlas Task does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information stored within the application. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the contact information provided on Google Play.
